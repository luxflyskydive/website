What's included and add-ons

In every package

  • Full flight suit, helmet, goggles, earplugs
  • Pre-flight safety briefing with instructor
  • Qualified instructor inside the tunnel
  • HighFly experience with your instructor
  • Professional souvenir photos
  • Instructor demonstration
  • Tour of The Art of Skydiving museum

Optional add-ons

  • Private coaching (see coaches page)
  • Group catering available
  • Loyalty points on every euro spent
  • Gift vouchers for any package

Not included

  • Private coaching (book separately)
  • Food and beverages
  • Merchandise or souvenirs
