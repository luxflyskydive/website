Strengthen your team. Together in freefall.

There's nothing like an experience that gets everyone out of their comfort zone at the same time.

Why it works for business

Levels the playing field

The CEO and the intern are both beginners in the tunnel. No hierarchy, no politics — just people figuring it out together. That dynamic carries back to the office.

Everyone actually participates

Unlike most extreme activities, indoor skydiving works for every age and fitness level. There's no one sitting it out on the sidelines.

Scales from 8 to 200+

Small leadership offsite or company-wide event — we build the programme around your numbers. Multiple tunnel lanes mean large groups flow without waiting around.

A full venue, not just an activity

Meeting rooms, catering, a bar with tunnel views, and full AV — use Luxfly as your entire day, from morning brief to evening debrief.

They'll talk about it for months

Most corporate events are forgotten by Thursday. The shared adrenaline and the photos from the tunnel stay with people long after the day is done.

Built-in return value

Teams that flew together last year can advance to more complex formations this year. Most of our corporate clients come back annually — the activity grows with the team.

What a corporate day looks like

09:30

Arrival, coffee, welcome brief in the meeting room. We set the tone and answer any questions about the activity.

10:00

Safety briefing and gear-up. Everyone gets suited up and ready. Our instructors go through safety and technique.

10:30

First group begins flying (rotating groups). While some fly, others watch or relax. Everyone gets their time.

12:30

Lunch on site. We can provide catering or you can bring your own. There's plenty of time to debrief and recharge.

13:30

Second activity block or informal workshop. More flying time or a structured debrief session in the meeting room.

15:00

Debrief and open tunnel time. Anyone who wants to fly again gets another go. A chance to wind down together.

16:00

End of programme. Plenty of time for showers and changing. Everyone heads home with memories and sore cheeks from smiling.
