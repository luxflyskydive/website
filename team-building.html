Strengthen your team. Together in freefall.

There's nothing like an experience that gets everyone out of their comfort zone at the same time.

Why it works for business

Levels the playing field

Indoor skydiving is new to almost everyone. Regardless of role or seniority, every participant starts from the same point — creating a genuine shared experience that strengthens team bonds.

Everyone actually participates

Unlike most extreme activities, indoor skydiving works for every age and fitness level. There's no one sitting it out on the sidelines.

Scales from 8 to 200+

Small leadership offsite or company-wide event — we build the programme around your numbers. Rotating groups ensure every participant gets their time in the tunnel without long waits.

A full venue, not just an activity

Meeting rooms, catering, a bar with tunnel views, and full AV — use Luxfly as your entire day, from morning brief to evening debrief.

They'll talk about it for months

Indoor skydiving creates a shared memory that lasts. The experience and the photos from the tunnel stay with people long after the day is done.

Built-in return value

Teams that flew together last year can advance to more complex formations this year. Most of our corporate clients come back annually — the activity grows with the team.

What's always included

  • Dedicated event coordinator from first contact
  • Pre-event briefing packs for participants
  • Full flight gear for all participants
  • Exclusive tunnel time during your session
  • Private briefing room
  • Instructor allocation (typically 1 per 6 flyers)
  • Viewing gallery access
  • Post-flight debrief space

Add-ons available

Meeting rooms, catering, professional photography, branded merchandise, and more can be arranged depending on your event. Pricing is tailored to your group size and requirements.
