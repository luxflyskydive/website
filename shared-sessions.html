Shared Sessions

More flying time. Better price.

Structured group sessions for flyers who are past the beginner stage and want regular, affordable tunnel time.

Flying with others.

You fly with other flyers in the same tunnel slot. You each get 1–2 minutes of air time, rotating. It's the most social way to fly — and the best value.

It's a completely different experience to solo tunnel time. You pick up things by observing others. The group dynamic pushes you harder. You see problem-solving in real time. And you make friends who speak your language — the language of flight.

This is where most of Luxembourg's serious flyers cut their teeth.
