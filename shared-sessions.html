LUXFLYLOVERS

Fly more. Pay less. Belong.

LUXFLYLOVERS is Luxfly's club for flyers who want regular, affordable tunnel time and a community to grow with.

A club built around shared flying.

LUXFLYLOVERS brings together flyers of all levels in shared tunnel sessions — giving you more flight time at lower cost while connecting you with others who share your passion.

The club is open to everyone from first-timers to advanced flyers. Sessions are organised by level so you're always flying with people at a similar stage, with leaders who plan themed evenings, camps with external coaches, and special events throughout the year.

Each session earns you points that can be redeemed for flight time, discounts, and exclusive rewards.

A progression from level 1 to 11

LUXFLYLOVERS uses a structured level system so you always know where you are and what's next.

1–3

Foundations

Belly flying alongside an instructor. First shared sessions with coaching support.

4–7

Intermediate

Back flying, head-up, head-down positions. Formation flying and group skills.

8–11

Advanced

Dynamic flying, competition preparation. Push your personal limits at the highest level.
