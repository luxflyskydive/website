Sky adventure

Another way to conquer the sky.

Float above the Moselle Valley at sunrise. Gentle, serene, and completely different from freefall.

What is a hot air balloon ride?

A gentle ascent into the sky, floating peacefully above the landscape. You rise in the basket with 3-7 other guests and a professional pilot. The experience lasts 60-90 minutes. After landing, you'll enjoy champagne and breakfast with the crew.

It's the opposite of freefall — no speed, no noise, just silence and views that stretch to the horizon. Suitable for all ages and fitness levels.

Flights operate spring through autumn, weather permitting. Early mornings are preferred for stable conditions.

How to book

Pricing

Typically €180-€250 per person, depending on season and balloon operator.

Includes champagne breakfast and transfer from Luxfly

Requirements

No special fitness required. Height and weight limits apply. Pregnant passengers cannot fly.

Early starts required (typically 06:00-07:00 departure)

The ultimate sky day

Combine a hot air balloon ride with an indoor skydiving flight at Luxfly for the complete sky experience.

Morning balloon flight

Start early with a gentle float above the Moselle Valley. Champagne breakfast at landing.

Rest & prepare

Return to Luxfly. Relax at the bar, eat lunch, mentally prepare for flight.

Afternoon freefall

Experience the extreme opposite of floating — 200+ km/h vertical acceleration in the tunnel. Adrenaline rush included.

Celebrate

Debrief at the bar with friends and family. You've now experienced two sides of flight.
