What kind of flyer are you?

Answer a few questions to find out which package suits your flying style.

1. What's your biggest feeling about flying for the first time?

Nervous but excited — I want professional reassurance
I want to understand exactly how it works
Just let me get in the air and experience it
I want to do this with friends and share the moment

2. When learning something new, you typically:

Follow instructions exactly and ask questions
Read/research everything before you start
Jump in and figure it out as you go
Learn better with a group and shared energy

3. Your ideal flight experience includes:

Knowing I'm being properly coached for safety and form
Understanding the physics and how every detail works
The pure sensation and nothing else matters
Friends and family cheering me on

4. After your flight, you'd want to:

Debrief with your instructor and understand what you did
Review the footage and analyze your technique
Ride the adrenaline high and celebrate
Grab drinks with everyone who flew and swap stories

5. On a scale, how adventurous are you?

I like adventure, but with safety guardrails and guidance
I like to master skills methodically over time
I'm a thrill-seeker — bring on the intensity
I'm game for anything if my friends are doing it too
