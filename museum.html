Interactive Museum

The Art of Skydiving

Dive into the fascinating world of skydiving and wind tunnel flying at The Art of Skydiving — the unique museum at the heart of Luxfly. This interactive museum invites you to explore the history, achievements, and evolution of skydiving, while showcasing the art and passion of flight.

Whether you're a skydiving enthusiast or simply curious, the museum offers a full immersion into the world of flight — celebrating its beauty, art, and history.

Access

Included with every flight package

A visit to The Art of Skydiving is included in all our flight packages. Explore the museum before or after your session — no extra charge.

Not flying? You're still welcome.

Museum-only entry is available for enquire at reception. Come for the exhibition — stay for the bar and the view of the tunnel.

Book a flight + museum

What's inside

Three permanent collections trace the full arc of freefall — from the first experiments to today's world champions.

Historical freefall exhibitions

Relive key moments in the history of skydiving — from the very first jumps to recent innovations in wind tunnel flying. Artefacts, documents, and immersive installations.

Art & photography

Artworks, sculptures, and photography inspired by the freedom of flight — where visual art meets the sport of skydiving.

Portraits of legends

Discover the stories and achievements of the world's greatest skydivers and flyers — including tributes to icons like Patrick de Gayardon.

When to visit

The museum is open during all Luxfly opening hours — no separate booking needed.

Wed – Fri: 12:00 – 20:00  ·  Sat – Sun: 10:00 – 19:00  ·  Mon & Tue: closed
