Using your voucher

Gift vouchers are simple to use. The recipient goes to shop.indoorskydive.lu, books their session, and enters the voucher code at checkout. If the session costs more than the voucher value, they pay the difference.

  1. Book a session — Visit shop.indoorskydive.lu and choose flight time, package, and preferred date
  2. Enter voucher code — At checkout, paste the voucher code in the "Gift Voucher" field
  3. Complete booking — The voucher amount is applied. If needed, pay any remaining balance by card at checkout or at reception on the day
  4. Show up and fly — Present confirmation details when you arrive

Voucher codes are unique to each voucher. They can be transferred or given to another person — vouchers aren't tied to a specific name. Valid for 12 months from purchase. To redeem a voucher code starting with "Z", enter it directly on the booking page. For other codes, email hello@luxfly.eu with your code and preferred date and time.
