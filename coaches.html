Coaching

Learn from people who live it.

Luxfly's coaches are IBA-certified and active competitors. They've spent thousands of hours in wind tunnels.

More than instruction.

There's a difference between an instructor and a coach. An instructor gets you safe. A coach gets you better.

At Luxfly, all our coaches are qualified instructors. But not all instructors are coaches. The coaching team works specifically with flyers who want to progress — from intermediate all the way to competition level. They watch for micro-adjustments. They know what to correct and what to leave alone. They're patient but demanding.

That's what makes the difference between spinning in the tunnel and actually improving.
