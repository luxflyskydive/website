Pathway

Start here. Fly forever.

From your first flight to competition level. The progression pathway at Luxfly is structured, achievable, and genuinely fun.

Everyone starts somewhere.

The pro flyers you see performing effortless head-down work all started in the same place. They stood in a briefing room. They put on a flight suit. They spent their first session looking nothing like what they expected.

The difference is they came back. And again. And again. Progression is the natural outcome of regular tunnel time coupled with good instruction. Most recreational flyers plateau because they don't fly often enough.

The progression pathway at Luxfly is designed to take a curious first-timer to a confident, competent tunnel flyer — on a realistic timeline. It's achievable for anyone willing to put in the hours.

Month 1

Discovery — stable arch, safe flight, body awareness

Month 2–3

Building Position — learning transitions

Month 4–6

Freefly Fundamentals — head-up position

Month 6–12

Coached Progression — competition preparation (optional)

Everyone starts in the same place. You just have to be willing to fall.

Progression roadmap

1. Beginner

Stable neutral position, hand signals, safe exit and entry. First 2–3 visits.

2. Intermediate

Controlled movement in arch position, basic turns, drift correction. 10–30 minutes total.

3. Advanced

Sitting, standing, transitions from belly to sit. Introduction to freefly. 30–60 minutes.

4. Competitive

Head-up, head-down approach, sequential transitions. Entry point to competitive flying. 60–150 minutes.

5. Pro

Dynamic sequences, 2-way formation, speed work, freestyle. Regional competition level. 150+ minutes.

The IBA tunnel certification system

Luxfly coaches can certify flyers under the IBA (International Bodyflight Association) system. IBA certifications are internationally recognised and open doors to flying at other tunnels worldwide with coaching credit. Your progress is recorded and portable.

Ask your coach about starting your IBA record at your next session.
