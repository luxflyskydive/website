Becoming a Proflyer

Your path to certified pro status.

The Hatch Pack and Human Flight programme turn passionate flyers into certified Proflyers — with a diploma and lifelong discounted rates.

The Hatch Pack & Human Flight

The Hatch Pack and Human Flight are certification programmes that grant you an official Proflyer diploma. Open to anyone who has completed a discovery flight at Luxfly, the programme guides you through all the fundamentals of indoor skydiving at your own pace.

Once certified, you unlock lifelong access to Proflyer rates — with up to 85% off your future flight sessions compared to standard prices. You also gain access to dedicated Proflyer time slots and become part of the LUXFLYLOVERS community.

The programme is designed for people who want more than an occasional visit — it's for flyers who want to genuinely improve and make the tunnel a regular part of their life.

Who can join

Anyone who has completed a discovery flight at Luxfly

What you earn

An official Proflyer diploma and certified status

Discount

Up to 85% off future flight sessions — for life

Community

Access to LUXFLYLOVERS shared sessions and the Proflyer network

Why become a Proflyer

Lifelong discounted rates

Once certified, your Proflyer rate is yours permanently. The more you fly, the more you save.

Flexible access

Plan sessions around your schedule with time slots reserved specifically for Proflyers.

Personalised coaching

Work with certified instructors who know advanced flight techniques and can sharpen your skills.

Exclusive events

Access to competitions, training camps, and events organised specifically for the Proflyer community.

Shared sessions

Share tunnel time with other Proflyers through LUXFLYLOVERS — more air time, lower cost.

A real community

Join a group of passionate flyers who exchange tips, share experiences, and grow together.

Already a skydiver?

If you're an experienced outdoor skydiver, you may be eligible for direct access to Proflyer rates without going through the full Hatch Pack programme. Get in touch and we'll guide you through the process.
