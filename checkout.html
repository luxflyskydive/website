Before You Book

Conditions & Requirements

Physical Requirements

  • Minimum age: 4 years old
  • Minimum weight: 15 kg
  • Maximum weight: 120 kg
  • Children under 14 must be accompanied
  • No recent back or neck injuries
  • Not suitable during pregnancy

What to Wear

  • Comfortable, fitted clothing
  • Lace-up athletic shoes required
  • No skirts, dresses or loose clothing
  • Long hair must be tied back
  • Remove jewellery and piercings
  • All gear provided on-site

Booking & Payment

  • Online payment required to confirm
  • Free cancellation up to 48 hours before
  • Gift vouchers accepted at checkout
  • Group bookings: contact us for a quote
  • All prices include VAT

On the Day

  • Arrive 20 minutes before your session
  • Check in at reception with booking ref
  • Briefing & gear fitting on arrival
  • Spectators welcome, free of charge
  • Bar & lounge available on-site
Accepted Payments

Secure payment at checkout. No hidden booking fees — the price shown is the price paid.

Good to know
Before you book, please read:
  • Ready to fly? Ages 4 and up are welcome! We recommend an adult accompany children for the adventure — add an adult formula to your reservation.
  • If you have a history of shoulder dislocation, unfortunately this experience is not for you.
  • The maximum weight for flying is 120 kg.
  • No alcohol or illicit substances before flying.
  • Please arrive 1 hour before your flight time for preparation and training.
  • Recommended attire: comfortable and casual, like jeans and sneakers. All equipment is provided.
  • Please note, your booking is subject to strict cancellation rules. If you're unsure about your date, consider opting for our Flexibility Insurance.
