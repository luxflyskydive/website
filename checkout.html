Before You Fly

Conditions & Requirements

Medical & Physical Requirements
—Maximum weight for all participants:  120 kg
—You cannot fly if you are pregnant
—No history of shoulder dislocation or trauma. If you have had a shoulder injury, this activity is unfortunately not for you.
—You must not be under the influence of alcohol or drugs
—If you have questions about these conditions, please contact us before booking: hello@luxfly.eu
On the Day
—Arrive  1 hour before  your flight time for check-in, briefing, and equipment fitting
—Dress code: jeans, T-shirt, trainers. We provide the rest (suit, helmet, goggles)
—All minors must be accompanied by an adult
—Spectators welcome — Bar & Lounge available for non-flyers
—Minimum age: 4 years old (adult accompaniment strongly recommended for children)
Important Notes at Booking
—Children aged 4+ can fly. We strongly recommend an adult accompany them — add an adult package to your order.
—Your booking is subject to strict cancellation rules. If you are unsure of your date, select the Flexibility Insurance add-on.
—Promo codes and gift vouchers can be entered at checkout (not for flight codes — use "Use a Gift Voucher"; not for GL codes — write to hello@luxfly.eu)
—Do not use an @pt.lu email address when creating your booking account.

How It Works

Booking in 4 steps
