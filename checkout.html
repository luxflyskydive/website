Before You Fly

Flight Conditions

All participants must meet these requirements.

Safety Requirements

  • Arrive 1 hour before your scheduled flight
  • No flying if pregnant
  • No flying under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Maximum weight: 120 kg
  • No recent shoulder trauma — contact us if unsure
  • All minors must be accompanied by an adult

Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation / reschedule up to 48h before (without insurance)
  • €15/person — 24h to 48h before flight
  • €30/person — within 24h of flight
  • With Flexibility Insurance: free reschedule up to 24h, €35/person within 24h
  • Gift vouchers valid 12 months from purchase

What to Wear

  • Jeans or comfortable trousers
  • T-shirt or light top
  • Trainers / sneakers
  • We provide flight suit, helmet and goggles
  • Remove jewellery and loose items before flying

How to Book

  • Click Book Now on any package above
  • Select a date from the live calendar, or receive a 12-month gift voucher by email
  • Present yourself 1 hour before your flight time
  • Questions? hello@luxfly.eu or +32 63 39 11 63
