Kids & Families

For the brave ones. From age four.

The world's most fun activity for kids. Genuinely safe. Genuinely incredible.

Safe. Certified. Tested.

Our facility meets European safety standards for wind tunnel operations. Every instructor is certified in child-specific flight instruction. We adjust wind speeds for smaller flyers and ensure proper body positioning at all times.

Kids actually adapt to the air faster than adults. They're lighter, they follow instructions precisely, and they instinctively understand how to move their bodies to stay stable. Most kids leave asking when they can come back.

A parent or legal guardian must be present at all times. Kids under 18 require signed parental consent. We have age-appropriate gear for all sizes from 4 years old onwards.

Parents

What parents need to know.

Children must be accompanied to the facility by a parent or legal guardian at all times. Under-18s require a signed waiver from a guardian before participation.

Kids under age 4 cannot fly, but are welcome to watch from our viewing area. We have age-appropriate safety considerations for all health conditions.

  • Minimum age 4
  • All gear is sized specifically for small flyers
  • Instructors have child-flight certification
  • No recent surgery or balance disorders
  • Parent/guardian must sign consent form
