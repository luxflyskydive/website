Conditions of participation.

Please read before booking. These conditions ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Health & fitness requirements

Who can fly

  • In general good health
  • No acute illness or infection
  • No severe vertigo or balance disorders
  • No uncontrolled fear of heights
  • Able to follow verbal and visual instructions
  • Can move limbs independently

Must consult a doctor first

  • Back or neck injuries in last 12 months
  • Heart condition or cardiac disease
  • Epilepsy or seizure disorder
  • Recent shoulder dislocation
  • Pregnancy (any trimester)
  • Severe asthma or respiratory condition
  • High blood pressure (uncontrolled)

If in doubt, consult your GP. Reception staff can advise on a case-by-case basis.

Age & height requirements

Minimum age: 4 years old. We have successfully flown children as young as 4 and participants as old as 95.

Weight limit: Flying is not recommended for individuals over 120 kg or those with a history of shoulder dislocation.

Under 18 requirements: Children and teenagers must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian. A signed waiver is required from a guardian before participation. Guardians are responsible for ensuring health clearance.

Waiver required: All participants regardless of age must sign a liability waiver before flying. For minors, a parent or legal guardian must sign on their behalf.

Cancellations & changes

Full refund: More than 48 hours before your session. No questions asked.

50% refund: Between 24-48 hours before your session. You'll receive 50% of the booking amount.

No refund: Less than 24 hours before your session. If you need to cancel with short notice, you forfeit the full amount.

Rescheduling: Free of charge with 48 hours' notice. Move your booking to any available date at no cost.

No-shows: If you don't arrive for your booked session without cancelling, you will be charged the full amount.

Force majeure: Illness, injury, or documented emergency with medical evidence may be handled case-by-case. Contact us immediately if circumstances prevent attendance.

What to wear & bring

What to wear

  • Comfortable, close-fitting clothes
  • Jeans, leggings, or tracksuit bottoms
  • Lace-up shoes or trainers (mandatory)
  • Hair tied back if shoulder-length or longer
  • Clothes that let you move freely

Don't wear into the tunnel

  • Scarves or loose neckwear
  • Loose jewellery or hair clips
  • Watches or bulky rings
  • Loose items in pockets — remove everything before entry
  • Anything that can catch the wind
  • Hats or hoods (not secured)

Secure lockers are provided for all valuables. Glasses are fine — we have larger goggles that fit over them. Anything not secured will be removed from the tunnel before entry.

House rules & facility guidelines

  • Follow all instructor instructions at all times without exception
  • No food or drink inside the tunnel or gear room (water bottle in waiting area is fine)
  • No alcohol or recreational substances before flying
  • No personal photography inside the tunnel (professional photos available)
  • Be respectful of other flyers' time and space
  • Children under 4 are welcome to watch from the viewing area but cannot enter the tunnel
  • Report any physical discomfort or concern immediately to your instructor
  • Do not attempt tricks or moves not approved by your instructor
  • Smoking is prohibited throughout the facility
  • Lost or damaged personal items are not the responsibility of Luxfly

Liability & waivers

By participating in indoor skydiving, you acknowledge the inherent risks of the activity. While our facility operates to the highest safety standards and all instructors are fully certified, accidents can occur. All participants must sign a comprehensive liability waiver accepting these risks. For minors, the parent or legal guardian assumes full responsibility.

Luxfly is insured for recreational flight activities. Participants are encouraged to check their own travel or sports insurance coverage before booking.

Questions or concerns?

If you have any questions about these conditions, please contact us directly. Our team can provide clarification on any policy or discuss individual circumstances that may require special consideration. Phone number and email are available on our contact page.
