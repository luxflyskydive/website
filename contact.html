Find us

Luxfly Indoor Skydive S.A.

Rue De Grass 103

6700 Sterpenich – Luxembourg, Belgium

By car: E411 (Autoroute du Soleil), exit 33 Sterpenich. Follow signs to Sterpenich / Grass, next to IKEA and Decathlon.

By public transport: Train to Arlon or Kleinbettingen, then TEC bus line 222 (direction Steinfort/IKEA) — 5 min walk to LUXFLY.

By bike: Bike paths from Arlon and Steinfort lead directly to us. Bicycle parking on site.
