Bachelor & Hen

The best stag party in Luxembourg.

No golf. No paint guns. Just 350 km/h of genuine madness. You're welcome.

Because they deserve a real send-off.

This is the ultimate pre-wedding activity — something everyone will actually remember. It works for groups of 3 to 14 people. Your stag or hen gets special treatment throughout — superhero costume included — and walks away with a certificate on the day. And then you all go to the bar and celebrate properly.

Flying is genuinely memorable. You'll see your best friends absolutely terrified, then absolutely exhilarated, all in about 30 seconds. That's a moment worth having. No cheesy games. No borrowed tuxedo. Just pure adrenaline and stories that last forever.

We've hosted many bachelor and hen parties, from small groups to large celebrations. This is your event, and we'll make sure it's unforgettable.
