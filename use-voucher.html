Gift Vouchers

Redeeming your voucher.

Three steps from code to flight.

How to use your voucher

01

Go to shop.indoorskydive.lu

Visit our booking site and choose your flight package. Select your preferred date and time. All packages are available: Discovery, Sensation, Performance, Progression, and Pro Sessions. You can also book kids packages or event group bookings if applicable.

02

Enter your voucher code

At checkout, you'll see a "Gift Voucher" field. Paste your voucher code there. The value is automatically applied to your booking. If your booking costs more than your voucher, you'll pay the difference by card at checkout or at reception on the day.

03

Complete your booking

Finish the checkout process and you'll receive a confirmation email with all the details. Show up on the day with your confirmation. You're booked, ready, and about to fly.

Common questions

Can I split a voucher across multiple sessions?

No. Each voucher code can only be used once, in a single transaction. If you have multiple vouchers, each one applies to a separate booking.

What if my voucher doesn't cover the full cost?

No problem. You can pay the remainder by card at checkout during booking, or in cash at reception on the day of your session. The voucher applies as a credit toward the total.

Does my voucher expire?

Gift vouchers are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. If you cannot use yours before it expires, you can renew it for a further 3 months — enter your code on the website and follow the steps to renew.

Can I transfer my voucher to someone else?

Yes. Vouchers are not tied to a specific name. Whoever has the voucher code can use it. You can give it to a friend or family member and they can book and redeem it themselves.

What if I lose my voucher code?

Contact us with proof of purchase (like your original receipt or order email) and we can reissue your code. We keep records of all voucher sales.

Can I get a cash refund on my voucher?

No. Vouchers are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash. They are valid for 12 months from purchase. If you can't use it yourself, you can gift it to someone else — vouchers are not tied to a name.

Ready to fly?

Your voucher is waiting to be used. Head to shop.indoorskydive.lu and book your session now. Choose your package, enter your code, and prepare for the most incredible experience of your life.
