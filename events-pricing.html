Minimum requirements

Group size for rates

Minimum 10 participants to qualify for group pricing discounts.

Advance booking

7 days minimum. 14 days recommended for optimal scheduling flexibility.

Deposit

30% due at time of booking to secure your date and time slot.

Balance payment

Full balance due 7 days before your event. We accept all major payment methods.

Exclusive tunnel hire

For large events of 75+ people, it's possible to book the tunnel exclusively for your group — no other members of the public flying during your event. This gives maximum flexibility on timing and is available from Monday to Thursday at premium rates. Exclusive hire packages include dedicated event management, staggered flying rotations optimized for your group size, and a flexible schedule that works around your other activities. Contact us directly for exclusive hire pricing and availability.
