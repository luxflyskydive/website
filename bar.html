The Bar

Fly first. Drink after.

The Luxfly terrace bar. Views straight into the tunnel. Cold beer. Good food.

A proper bar.

The terrace is built around the viewing window into the tunnel. You sit with a drink and watch people fly. Occasionally you recognise someone. Usually there's cheering.

Simple food, honest drinks. Watch the flyers from the terrace while you wait for your round.

Open during centre hours, Wed–Sun. Most afternoons there's someone from the day's sessions still here, telling the story of their flight.
