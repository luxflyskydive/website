The birthday they won't stop talking about.

We handle the excitement. You handle the cake.

Ditch the party room.

There's cake, there's a party room, and then there's flying. Luxfly hosts private birthday sessions for kids and adults. Your group has a dedicated instructor, a private briefing room, and flight time that goes at your pace. Whether you're eight or eighty — flying is the most memorable birthday gift there is.

Every birthday package includes full use of our briefing room, a qualified birthday instructor, and flight time tailored to your group's age and experience level. Non-flying guests are welcome to watch from our viewing area. We can handle everything from intimate celebrations to large group parties.

Minimum group: 5 people. Available Wednesday through Sunday, subject to availability. Contact us at least one week in advance to book your celebration.

Kids & Adults

Works for every age.

Kids from age 4 through adults up to 95. We've hosted birthday parties for five-year-olds and sixty-year-olds and the smiles are exactly the same. Each session is calibrated to the group's age and experience level.

Children's parties are supervised by parents as well as instructors. Adult parties can be as competitive or as relaxed as you'd like. We've done celebrations for brides, grooms, milestone birthdays, and milestone ages. Every birthday is special here.

How to book a birthday

01

Get in touch

Call us or fill in the quote form at least 7 days in advance. Tell us the birthday person's age and group size.

02

Choose your package

Select your flight package and any add-ons you'd like. Confirm your preferred date and time.

03

Pay the deposit

Secure your date with a 50% deposit. Balance is due 7 days before the party.

04

Arrive 15 minutes early, gear up, brief, and celebrate in the air. Unforgettable memories guaranteed.
