Parents often ask…

What is the minimum age?

4 years old. Children fly in specially adapted conditions — wind speed is set according to their size and weight. Our instructors are experienced with young flyers of all ages.

How long is the session?

A typical birthday party lasts around 2 hours from arrival to departure — including the safety briefing, equipment fitting, flight time, and use of the birthday room.

Is it safe?

Yes. Every participant is fully geared, briefed, and supervised by a qualified instructor throughout the flight. Luxfly operates at the highest safety standards and welcomes children from age 4.

Can parents fly too?

Absolutely. Parents and adults who want to fly alongside the kids are very welcome — simply add adult flights to the booking. Non-flying guests can watch from the viewing gallery.

How many children do we need?

A minimum of 6 participants per booking. There is no maximum — for larger groups we schedule accordingly. A discount applies automatically from the 7th participant on weekends.

Where are you located?

Rue de Grass 103, 6700 Sterpenich, Belgium — easily accessible from Luxembourg city, Arlon, and the Greater Region. Free parking available on site.

What should children wear?

Comfortable clothing and lace-up shoes that cannot come off. We provide all flight equipment including a suit, helmet, and goggles. No jewellery during the flight.

What is HighFly?

HighFly is a premium upgrade (+€15/child) where your child flies higher in the tunnel with the instructor alongside them the entire time. Strongly recommended — most groups add it once they hear about it.
