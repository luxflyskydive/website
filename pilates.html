Fitness

Body awareness. In the air.

Sky Pilates combines the precision of Pilates with the unique challenge of the wind tunnel.

What is Sky Pilates?

Sky Pilates is a structured aerial fitness programme that applies Pilates principles — controlled movement, core strength, breath — to indoor skydiving. A certified instructor guides you through movements in the tunnel, focusing on alignment, balance, and strength.

You're suspended in the airflow, your full bodyweight supported by the wind. The instructor stands outside the tunnel, cueing movements that engage your core, improve stability, and build strength in ways ground-based exercise cannot replicate.

It's challenging, meditative, and oddly gentle — no impact, no joint stress, pure proprioceptive awareness.

Benefits

Core strength

The wind demands constant micro-adjustments. Your deep stabiliser muscles work continuously, building core strength more effectively than traditional Pilates.

Balance & proprioception

Flying requires constant spatial awareness. Sky Pilates sharpens your sense of where your body is and how to control it in three dimensions.

Zero impact

No pounding joints, no compression. Ideal for people with arthritis or joint concerns who still want challenging exercise.

Flexibility gains

Controlled stretching in weightlessness safely improves range of motion without the risk of traditional stretching.

Mind-body connection

Floating demands presence. The meditative quality of Pilates meets the full-body engagement of flying for deep mind-body awareness.

Posture improvement

Pilates trains alignment and postural awareness. Sky Pilates locks in these improvements through dynamic control in the air.

Class schedule & booking

Who it's for

Pilates enthusiasts

Already practising Pilates? Sky Pilates takes your practice to the next level with new challenges and deeper engagement.

Fitness seekers

Want something different from the gym? Sky Pilates is low-impact, high-engagement, and genuinely unique.

Injury recovery

Rehabbing after injury? Discuss with your physiotherapist, but zero-impact flying may be ideal for controlled recovery.

Mindful exercisers

Love the meditative aspect of Pilates? Sky Pilates deepens that mind-body connection in a profound way.
