Come fly with us.

We're looking for people who love what they do. Sound familiar?

What it's like to work here.

Luxfly is a small team. Everyone knows everyone. There's no hiding and there's no politics. You're either good at your job or you're not — and if you're not, we'll help you get there.

We fly, most of us. Some of us came here because of the sport and stayed because of the team. The facility is new, the equipment is excellent, and the people who walk through the door every day are genuinely excited to be here. That energy is real and it makes the work good.

There's room to grow, but growth means becoming better at what you do, not climbing a ladder.

Full-time / Part-time

Safety & Technical Officer

Full-time

Background in mechanical or electrical engineering, experience with high-velocity mechanical systems. Training provided on tunnel-specific systems.
