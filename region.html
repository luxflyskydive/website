Small country. Big air.

Luxfly is located in the heart of Europe. 20 minutes from Luxembourg City. Easy to reach from Belgium, France, and Germany.

Where we are

Luxfly is located in Sterpenich, Belgium — just 300 metres from the Luxembourg border and 7 km from Arlon. The facility sits next to IKEA and Decathlon, making it easy to find and simple to reach from both Luxembourg and Belgium.

By car: take the E411 (Autoroute du Soleil), exit 33 Sterpenich. Follow signs for the Sterpenich / Grass site. Free parking on site.

How to get here

By car

E411 (Autoroute du Soleil), exit 33 Sterpenich. Follow signs to Sterpenich / Grass site, next to IKEA and Decathlon. 7 km from Arlon, 300 m from the Luxembourg border.

Address: Rue De Grass 103, 6700 Sterpenich, Belgium

By bus

Train to Arlon or Kleinbettingen, then TEC bus line 222 direction Steinfort/IKEA — 5 minute walk from the stop. Direct bus lines also run from Charleroi (via Flibco or TEC) with stops near Arlon or Luxembourg.

Check tec.be for current schedules

Luxembourg as a destination

Luxembourg is the smallest capital in the EU but one of the most visited per capita. It's a perfect base for exploring Western Europe.

The city

Luxembourg City is compact, walkable, and built on dramatic elevation changes. The Old Town is UNESCO-listed. Medieval fortifications rise above the Alzette River. Museums, galleries, cafes, and restaurants fill the narrow streets.

Flight + culture: arrive in the city, explore for the morning, fly at Luxfly in the afternoon, return to the city for dinner.

The region

The Moselle wine region is 30 minutes south. The Ardennes forest and Vianden Castle are accessible from Luxembourg. Belgium and Germany are close — day trips are straightforward.

Luxfly fits naturally into a longer exploration of the greater region.

Where to stay

Luxury hotels

4-star properties in Luxembourg City. Most offer spa and wellness facilities. Many are within 20 minutes of Luxfly.

Mid-range

Good quality 3-star hotels in the city and surrounding towns. Strong value. Family-friendly options available.

Budget & hostels

Hostel culture is strong in Luxembourg. Well-managed, social, and centrally located. Great for young travellers.

Rural accommodation

Guesthouses and farm stays in the Ardennes and Moselle valleys. Peaceful, scenic, easy day trip to Luxfly.
