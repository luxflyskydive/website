Weekly sharing sessions

Our weekly sharing sessions are open to all levels and disciplines — from first-time Proflyers through to advanced Head-Down and Sequential competitors. Some sessions are coached by experts to help you refine specific skills and progress faster.

Each group has its own WhatsApp chat — join to stay updated on schedules, changes, and who's flying that week.

Sessions combine fun, structured learning, and plenty of air time. It's the fastest way to improve while building real connections with other flyers.
