Built for groups of all sizes

2–250

Luxfly is designed to accommodate groups of any size. Whether you're bringing 2 people or 250, we have the infrastructure, expertise, and flexibility to make your event work seamlessly.

Every group gets a dedicated event coordinator from first contact to the day after. We handle pre-event communication, briefing packs, flexible time slots, and can arrange exclusive tunnel hire for larger events where you want complete control.

Our two-lane facility means we can run multiple groups simultaneously or give your entire party exclusive access. The choice is yours, and we'll adapt the programme to match your vision.

What you get

  • Meeting rooms available
  • Catering on site
  • 2 tunnel lanes
  • Full AV in meeting rooms
  • Dedicated event coordinator
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Locker facilities
  • Bar & terrace access
  • Airport transfers available
  • Branded merchandise options

The Space

Everything you need. Under one roof.

The 3,000m² Luxfly facility is purpose-built for events. It includes the wind tunnel, a dedicated events briefing area, a 60-person meeting room with AV capabilities, a terrace bar with views into the tunnel, catering facilities for groups up to 250, and comprehensive locker rooms. Everything from airport transfers to custom branded merchandise can be arranged for larger events. One facility. Everything you need.
