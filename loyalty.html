Loyalty

Fly more. Pay less.

Earn points every time you fly. Redeem them for free flights and discounts. Your phone number is your loyalty card.

"Sign up at reception in seconds. Your phone number is all you need."

How it works

The Luxfly loyalty programme runs through FidelityBox. Registration is free — just give your phone number at reception and it becomes your loyalty card. No app needed to sign up, no contracts, nothing to carry.

You earn points automatically every time you visit. How you earn depends on your profile: First Timer (newcomers and casual visitors) or SportFlyer (regular and training flyers). Both profiles are active on the same phone number.

Track your points and available rewards at any time through the free FidelityBox app, available on iOS and Android.
