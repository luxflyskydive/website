Loyalty

Fly more. Pay less.

The Luxfly loyalty card rewards regular flyers with better rates, priority booking, and exclusive perks.

"The more you fly, the less you pay. Simple."

One card. Unlimited flights.

You pay upfront for a block of tunnel time. Those minutes never expire. The more you buy, the better the per-minute rate. Once your card is loaded, you use those minutes booking by booking, session by session, with no rush and no pressure.

Your card is linked to your Luxfly account. Every time you book, minutes are deducted. You can share unused minutes with friends and family. No hidden fees, no expiry dates, no strings attached.

Regular flyers save significant money — especially at our higher tiers where the per-minute rate drops to €24 compared to €35-€40 per minute on pay-as-you-go sessions.
