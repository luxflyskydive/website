Booking is strongly recommended

To guarantee your spot and ensure the best experience, we recommend booking through our online platform before arriving. Walk-ins are welcome when slots are available.

While you're here

Our centre is open to everyone during these hours — not just flyers. Come to watch, visit the museum, or enjoy a drink at the bar.

The Art of Skydiving

Museum included with all flight packages. €15 standalone entry.

Bar

Open during all centre hours. Perfect for spectators and post-flight refreshments.

Observation area

Watch other flyers from the viewing area. Free for all visitors.

After-hours private hire

After-hours private hire available Mon–Thu from 07:00. Contact us to arrange.

When to book

Last sessions are admitted 90 minutes before closing time. If you're bringing a group of 5 or more, we recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred time slot.

Pro training programmes and shared sessions can be scheduled for times outside standard hours with advance notice.

Pro training

Monday–Thursday, early morning access (from 07:00) can be arranged for pro flyers and coaching sessions.

Contact pro@luxfly.lu to schedule dedicated training slots.
