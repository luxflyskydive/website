About Luxfly

Europe's tallest tunnel. Luxembourg's favourite thrill.

Born from a love of flight. Built for everyone.

Where it started.

Luxfly opened in Sterpenich, Belgium — right on the Luxembourg border — with a simple belief: that the sensation of freefall should be available to anyone, not just skydivers willing to jump out of planes. We built Europe's tallest indoor skydiving tunnel: 35 metres of vertical airflow, engineered to deliver the real freefall experience without altitude, without aircraft, and without the mandatory leap of faith.

Since opening, we've welcomed over 80,000 visitors. We've helped children as young as 4 take their first flight. We've trained national champions. We've hosted corporate events for 250 people and given solo birthday gifts to one very surprised person at a time.

The tunnel doesn't care about your age, your fitness level, or your fear of heights. It just wants to make you fly.

Our values

Safety first. Always.

No one flies here without a proper briefing. Every instructor is certified and stays in the tunnel with you. No exceptions.

Accessible to everyone.

Flying shouldn't be a privilege. We've worked hard to make our prices fair, our packages flexible, and our staff genuinely helpful.

Honest about what it is.

We won't tell you it's easy. It's not. It's challenging, exhilarating, and completely unlike anything else. That's the point.

Built to last.

We chose the best tunnel technology available. We maintain it obsessively. We want you to come back next year and find it exactly as good as the day you left.

The Team

The people behind the air.

Luxfly's team includes certified flight instructors, event coordinators, safety officers, and front-of-house staff who collectively have logged thousands of hours in tunnels around the world. Most of them fly themselves. All of them genuinely love what they do — and it shows.

When you come through the door, you're not meeting a franchise script. You're meeting people who chose to be here because they believe in what happens inside that tunnel.
