About Luxfly

Europe's tallest indoor skydiving tunnel.

Safe from age 4 to 99. Located in Sterpenich, Belgium — right on the Luxembourg border.

Who we are

Your destination for indoor freefall.

Luxfly is a wind tunnel located in Sterpenich, right on the Belgium-Luxembourg border. Our mission is to make the incredible sensation of freefall accessible to everyone — in a safe, professional, and welcoming environment.

The tunnel stands 35 metres tall with airflow up to 350 km/h — one of the tallest in the world. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced Proflyer, the experience is adapted to you.

Open from age 4 to 99, with no prior experience needed. Your instructor is in the tunnel with you from the very first second.

What's at Luxfly

The Wind Tunnel

A 35-metre vertical flight chamber with transparent walls — powered by 4 motors and built for the full freefall experience.

The Museum

The Art of Skydiving — an interactive museum dedicated to the history of parachuting and human flight. Open to all visitors.

The Bar

A bar with a direct view into the wind tunnel — the perfect place to watch flyers and unwind after your session.

The Amphitheatre

A dedicated viewing area where spectators can watch the flyers and share the experience with family or colleagues.

For everyone

Beginners

No experience needed. Your instructor flies in the tunnel with you from the very first second and guides you at every step.

Families & Kids

Safe from age 4. Birthday parties, family sessions, and kids' packages — with instructors who specialise in younger flyers.

Proflyers

Preferential rates and regular access for certified Proflyers. Coaching, shared sessions, and camp events available.

Companies

Team building and corporate events for groups from 2 to 250, with meeting rooms and catering through EVENTIFY.
