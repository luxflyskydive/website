About Luxfly

Europe's tallest indoor skydiving tunnel.

Safe from age 4 to 95. Located in Sterpenich, Belgium — right on the Luxembourg border.

Who we are

Your destination for indoor freefall.

Luxfly is a next-generation wind tunnel located in Sterpenich, near the Luxembourg border. Our mission is to make the incredible sensation of freefall accessible to everyone — in a safe, professional, and welcoming environment.

The tunnel stands 35 metres tall with airflow up to 350 km/h — one of the tallest and most powerful in the world. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced Proflyer, the experience is adapted to you.

We've welcomed thousands of visitors since opening — from children as young as 4 taking their first flight, to national-level competitors training for championships.

What's at Luxfly

The Wind Tunnel

A 35-metre vertical flight chamber with transparent walls — powered by 4 motors and built for the full freefall experience.

The Museum

The Art of Skydiving — an interactive museum dedicated to the history of parachuting and human flight. Open to all visitors.

The Bar

A warm bar with a direct view into the wind tunnel — the perfect place to watch flyers and unwind after your session.

The Amphitheatre

A dedicated viewing area where spectators can watch the flyers and share the experience with family or colleagues.

For everyone

Beginners

Experience indoor freefall with no prior experience needed. Fully guided by a qualified instructor in the tunnel with you.

Families & Kids

Safe from age 4. Birthday parties, family sessions, and kids' packages all available — with dedicated kids' instructors.

Proflyers

Preferential rates and regular access for certified Proflyers. Coaching, shared sessions, and competition preparation available.

Companies

Unforgettable team building and corporate events. Groups from 2 to 250, with meeting rooms and catering through EVENTIFY.

The Team

Passionate experts. Certified instructors.

Our team is made up of flight enthusiasts and qualified professionals — instructors, event coordinators, safety officers, and coaches who are committed to making every visit safe, enjoyable, and memorable.

Every certified instructor guides you step by step — so you can experience wind tunnel flying in a secure environment, whether it's your first time or your hundredth.
